# About Generalist Programmer

Welcome to Generalist Programmer, a platform sharing practical insights on technology leadership, software development, and real-world experiences building tech products.

## What We Cover

This site features in-depth articles and case studies on:

- **Technology Leadership & Strategy** - CTO services, team building, strategic decision-making
- **Software Development** - Architecture, security, databases, and best practices
- **Real-World Case Studies** - Experiences building actual products and businesses
- **Product Guides** - Practical tools and services for developers and businesses

## Our Approach

We believe in sharing authentic, experience-based content. Our articles combine technical depth with practical insights from real projects, providing value to both technical and business audiences.

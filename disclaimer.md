# Affiliate Disclaimer

**Last Updated: November 18, 2025**

## Transparency and Disclosure

At Generalist Programmer, we believe in complete transparency with our readers. This page explains our affiliate relationships and how they may affect the content on our website.

## Affiliate Relationships

Generalist Programmer participates in various affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click on certain links on our website and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you.

### What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special tracking links that allow us to earn a commission when you purchase products or services through them. These links help support our website and allow us to continue creating free, high-quality content for our readers.

### Affiliate Programs We Participate In

We may be compensated through the following types of affiliate programs:

- **Amazon Associates Program:** We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.

- **Software and Tool Affiliates:** We maintain affiliate relationships with various software companies, development tools, hosting providers, and educational platforms.

- **Course and Educational Content:** We may earn commissions from online learning platforms and course creators.

- **Other Affiliate Networks:** We participate in various other affiliate networks and programs relevant to programming, development, and technology.

## FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines concerning the use of endorsements and testimonials in advertising, we are required to inform you of our affiliate relationships.

**Our Commitment:**
- We clearly label sponsored content and affiliate links where practical
- We disclose our affiliate relationships in accordance with FTC 16 CFR Part 255
- We maintain transparency about our monetization methods

## Our Editorial Independence

### Honest Recommendations

Our affiliate relationships do NOT influence our editorial content or recommendations. We maintain strict editorial independence and only recommend products, services, and resources that:

- We have personally used or thoroughly researched
- We genuinely believe provide value to our readers
- Are relevant to our audience's needs and interests
- Meet our quality standards

### No Pay-to-Play

We do not accept payment in exchange for positive reviews or recommendations. If we receive a product or service for free for review purposes, we will disclose this relationship.

### Balanced Coverage

We strive to provide balanced, honest assessments of products and services, including both strengths and weaknesses. The presence of an affiliate relationship does not guarantee a positive review.

## Your Purchase Decisions

### No Obligation

You are under no obligation to click on any affiliate links or make purchases through them. You can always navigate directly to a merchant's website if you prefer.

### Price Transparency

Using our affiliate links does NOT increase the price you pay. In many cases, we may negotiate special discounts or offers exclusively for our readers.

### Due Diligence

We encourage you to conduct your own research and due diligence before making any purchase decisions. Our recommendations are based on our experiences and opinions, which may differ from yours.

## Types of Compensation

We may receive compensation in the following forms:

- **Commission on Sales:** A percentage of sales made through our affiliate links
- **Cost Per Action (CPA):** Payment when you complete specific actions (e.g., sign up for a free trial)
- **Cost Per Click (CPC):** Payment when you click on certain links
- **Flat Fees:** Fixed payments for sponsored content or partnerships
- **Free Products/Services:** Complimentary access to products or services for review purposes

When we receive free products or services, or create sponsored content, we will clearly disclose this in the relevant article.

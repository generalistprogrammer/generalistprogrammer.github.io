## Write for Us

We're always looking for talented writers and developers to contribute to our community!

### Guest Contributor Guidelines

If you're passionate about programming, development practices, or tech in general, we'd love to feature your work. We accept guest posts on topics including:

- Programming tutorials and guides
- Software development best practices
- Career advice for developers
- Tool reviews and comparisons
- Industry insights and trends
- Personal development stories

**What We're Looking For:**
- Original, unpublished content (minimum 1,000 words)
- Practical, actionable advice backed by experience
- Clear, engaging writing style
- Properly formatted code examples (where applicable)
- Relevant images and diagrams (optional but encouraged)

**Interested?** Send us a pitch or draft to [contribute@generalistprogrammer.com](mailto:contribute@generalistprogrammer.com) with:
- A brief bio (2-3 sentences)
- Links to previous writing samples
- Your proposed topic or completed article

## Partnership Opportunities

We're open to exploring partnerships with like-minded brands and organizations.

### Collaboration Options

- **Sponsored Content:** High-quality sponsored articles that provide value to our readers
- **Product Reviews:** Honest reviews of developer tools, platforms, and services
- **Joint Ventures:** Co-created content, webinars, or educational initiatives
- **Affiliate Partnerships:** Long-term relationships with products we genuinely recommend

**Important:** We maintain strict editorial independence and only partner with products and services we believe in. All sponsored content is clearly labeled.

**Partnership Inquiries:** [partnerships@generalistprogrammer.com](mailto:partnerships@generalistprogrammer.com)
